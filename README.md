## 📁 Projects

### 🏦 Bank Customer Churn Prediction
`Python` `pandas` `scikit-learn` `XGBoost` `EDA`

Analysed customer behaviour data to identify key churn indicators. Built and compared multiple classification models — **Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, and XGBoost** — to predict customer attrition and surface actionable retention recommendations.

📂 [`bank_churn_prediction/`](./bank_churn_prediction)

---

### 😴 Sleep Disorder Analysis
`Python` `pandas` `Matplotlib` `Seaborn` `EDA`

Exploratory analysis of sleep health data to identify patterns and factors contributing to sleep disorders. Includes data cleaning, visualisation, and statistical insights across age groups, occupations, and lifestyle variables.

📂 [`sleep_disorder_analysis/`](./sleep_disorder_analysis)

---

### 🎵 SQL Music Store Analysis
`SQL` `MySQL` `Business Analysis`

Analysed a digital music store database using SQL to answer key business questions — top-selling genres, best customers, revenue by country, and artist performance. Demonstrates use of JOINs, window functions, CTEs, and aggregations.

📂 [`sql_music_store_analysis/`](./sql_music_store_analysis)

---

### 📈 Stock Market Analysis
`Python` `pandas` `Matplotlib` `Financial Analysis`

Analysed historical stock price data to identify trends, compare performance across companies, and visualise price movements. Includes moving averages, return analysis, and correlation between stocks.

📂 [`stock_analysis/`](./stock_analysis)

---

### 🏢 Top 1000 Companies Analysis
`Python` `pandas` `EDA` `Data Visualisation`

Exploratory analysis of the world's top 1000 companies by revenue. Investigated industry distribution, country-wise rankings, and revenue trends to derive business insights.

📂 [`top_1000_companies_analysis/`](./top_1000_companies_analysis)
